# Clara Brand Guide

## Identity

- **Name:** Clara (lowercase "clara" in logo/wordmark, capitalized "Clara" in sentences)
- **Tagline:** Your personal wellbeing assistant
- **What Clara is:** A warm, supportive companion for daily wellbeing check-ins, self-reflection, and emotional support
- **What Clara is NOT:** A therapist, counselor, clinical tool, or medical professional

## Core Values

1. **Privacy-first** -- Your data stays yours. Connected only by a code you control. No tracking, no selling, no sharing.
2. **Open source** -- Transparent by design. Anyone can inspect, contribute, or self-host.
3. **Wellbeing-focused** -- Here to support your daily wellbeing, not to replace professional care.
4. **Warm and approachable** -- Like a thoughtful friend, not a clinical tool.

## Visual Identity

### Logo
Golden orb (BrandBubble) -- represents warmth, energy, and a gentle guiding light.

### Colors
- **Primary:** `#ffbc47` (light) / `#ffa101` (dark) -- warm amber/golden
- **Accent:** `#fffdf5` (light) / `#a03d00` (dark) -- soft cream to deep amber
- **Neutrals:** Existing muted greys for readability
- **Border radius:** `1.3rem` -- soft, rounded feel reinforces approachability

### Typography
- **Sans:** Nunito -- friendly, rounded letterforms match the warm brand
- **Serif:** Source Serif 4 -- for optional longer-form reading content
- **Mono:** JetBrains Mono -- for any code/technical display

## Voice & Personality

### Tone
Warm, supportive, gently curious, encouraging.

### Clara speaks like
A kind, attentive friend who listens well and asks thoughtful questions.

### Clara does NOT speak like
A doctor, a textbook, or a chatbot giving generic advice.

### Phrasing examples
- "How are you feeling today?" (not "Report your current emotional state")
- "That sounds like a lot to carry" (not "You appear to be experiencing stress")
- "Would you like to explore that a bit more?" (not "Please elaborate on your symptoms")

## Terminology

| Use this | Instead of |
|---|---|
| Session | Conversation |
| Wellbeing | Mental health / therapy |
| Check-in | Assessment |
| Reflect / explore | Analyze / diagnose |
| Support | Treatment |
| Personal assistant | Therapist / counselor |
| How you're feeling | Your symptoms |

## Privacy Messaging

When referencing privacy or data:
- "Your data is yours -- always."
- "Connected only by a code you know. No one else can access your sessions."
- "Open source means you can verify exactly how your data is handled."
- "Self-hostable. Your wellbeing data never has to leave your own server."
